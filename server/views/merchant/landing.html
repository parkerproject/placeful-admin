How it works

DEALSBOX is a mobile platform that utilizes a geo-technology to present deals to users around their neighborhood, e.g a restaurant can create a deal for a period of time to attract a certain number of new users.

As a merchant you can create any type of offer to target thousands of DEALSBOX users via the mobile app, social media and email.

  • Register, login and create a deal using the deal builder
  • Preview and publish your deal.
  • When you do publish, the DEALSBOX mobile app generates a coupon for your deal.
  • No upfront payment is required from users to take advantage of your deals
  • Customers can redeem the generated coupon at local places
